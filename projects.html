Hierarchical Multi-Cluster RL for Epidemic Control

A hierarchical reinforcement learning framework for allocating limited testing and intervention resources across asynchronous clusters. The system combines a master policy for cross-cluster allocation with cluster-level decision models under resource constraints.

CovidEnv: Multi-Agent Test and Quarantine Environment

A multi-agent simulation environment for outbreak control with testing and quarantine actions. Designed for studying constrained objectives, policy robustness, and sensitivity to cost parameters.

SustainCluster: Carbon-Aware Datacenter Control

A hierarchical multi-agent RL system for sustainable datacenter scheduling. Models workload decisions and energy objectives to improve carbon efficiency under operational constraints.
