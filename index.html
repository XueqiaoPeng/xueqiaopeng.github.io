I am a Ph.D. candidate at The Ohio State University advised by Prof. Andrew Perrault. My research focuses on reinforcement learning for resource-constrained decision-making with applications in epidemic control and sustainable systems.

My recent work explores hierarchical multi-agent reinforcement learning, constrained optimization, and interpretable policy design for real-world social impact and infrastructure systems.
